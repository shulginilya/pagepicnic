Content

Adding and editing content is fast and simple. This gives you more time for the important things, like running your business.

Drag & drop

Our drag & drop feature is amazingly simple to use - anybody can do it! Simply drag a block from the content menu and drop it onto your page. It really is that simple.

Point & click to edit

To edit your website content, simply point and click on a content block. Making changes is simple and fast - no separate dashboards or html text.

20+ Elements

Along with your world-class template, PagePicnic offers a variety of different blocks on the content menu including text, images, video and online forms.

Customization tools

Every business is different and we understand that. That’s why we let you customise your website exactly the way you want to - at no extra cost.

Colors

Pick and choose just the right colours for all your headings, text, buttons and much more. With our colour picker you can also save your favourites.

Fonts

Choosing a font that best reflects your business is a pretty important element when building a website. You’ll have the freedom to choose from over 60 unique fonts. We’ve even sorted the fonts just to make the choice that little bit easier.

Backgrounds

Backgrounds don’t have to be boring. That’s why we give you the choice to not only select from custom colours or patterns but also free stock photos and videos. Still don’t like the selection? Then simply upload your own custom image!

Mobile

Having an awesome website means more than just offering a desktop version. Your customers also need to explore your website on mobile and other devices. We include a fully responsive design with all of our PagePicnic templates. You don’t even need to add or edit the content to your mobile site - it’s all done automatically. It couldn’t be easier!

And so much more

The drag-n-drop interface can be used by five year olds.

Image editing tools

PagePicnic offers two different image editors. One is designed for all your basic needs like cropping, overlays, adding filters and adding text. The other is an advanced image editor built for those graphic wizards.

Statistics

You’re going to want to measure all that new traffic to your awesome new website. Our statistics show you the most relevant and beneficial statistics to help you track activity across your site. You can of course also add Google Analytics to your website.

Interactive SEO guide

With our interative SEO guide, you’ll get simple step-by-step advice on how to make your website more search-engine friendly. No technical jargon, just plain English.

Maps

Want to show your visitors where they can find you? Adding a map has never been easier. No tricky plugins - just drag & drop, and add your address!

Audio player

Like everything else you can use the simple drag & drop to add your audio block to any page. Each audio file has a generous 300MB size limit.

Files & Documents

Being able to make files and documents available for viewing comes in handy which is why we included it in our content menu. Just drag & drop, and upload your files or documents. Easy!

Calendar

Our calendar let’s you keep everybody updated with upcoming events and gatherings. It’s nice and easy to use which gives you more time to plan the event.

Guestbook

Isn’t it nice to know when people enjoy your service? You’ll be able to do just that with the Guestbook feature and don’t worry, we’ll sort out the spam from the nice comments.

Comments

Want people share their thoughts on your website? Add a comment block with a simple drag & drop, and you’re on your way.

Multi-language support

Do you have international customers? You might need to provide multi-language support which can be easily customised with PagePicnic Professional packages.

RSS Feed Reader

Add an RSS feed reader to your webite so that subscribers never miss an update. There’s also a bunch of advanced settings which you can configure to your liking.

Password protected pages

Establishing and maintaining website security is of paramount importance. To assist you in doing so, PagePicnic offers password protected pages and subpages.

Custom HTML

Are you a bit of a html coding genious? Then you’re more than welcome to add your own custom html to your website by simply using our drag & drop block.

iPhone & Android app

As a business owner you’re used to being on the go all the time. Now you can use your precious time more effectively with our PagePicnic app by adding and editing website content directly.

Blog

After you have added a blog block to a page, you can also edit the layout to suit your needs. Simply drag & drop, then style as needed.
